Research

I do research in Machine Learning. My recent works focus on Graph Learning, especially to universally enhance the performance and scalability of Graph Neural Networks (GNNs). I have also worked in Adversarial Attacks/Defenses, Semi-supervised Learning, Meta Learning, and Medical Imaging. Below are my selected publications.
